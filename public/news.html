it's like an 'i love you' but cooler

a mirror shatters, and from the shards emerges it's like an 'i love you' but cooler, the debut single by georges, an artist pulling sound from the edge of rupture. sharp, erratic, and emotionally loaded, the track refuses to settle or behave.

across six relentless minutes, georges fractures the architecture of deconstructed club and noise, building a world that folds in on itself, unfolds again, and never lands where it started. the track consists of no verses, no choruses, but goes through multiple phases throughout its runtime.
